What is MOBone

MOBone - A species appropriate diet for Dogs.

A species appropriate nutrition is a diet that is appropriate for a specific species. In order to thrive, an animal must eat a diet that its species has evolved to eat – a diet that its body is able to consume and digest effectively.

MOBone is,

  • A Gold standard diet, closest to the evolutionary diet of dogs that is practically possible.
  • Made from human-grade fresh raw meaty bones, meats, organs, vegetables, turmeric and coconut oil, minimally processed, minced and frozen.
  • Free from synthetic supplements, additives, preservatives and most importantly carbohydrates.
  • Not made of discards from butcher shops/slaughter houses, grains/carbs in any form.
  • Formulated by certified canine nutritionists.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

DOGS ARE CAPTIVE CARNIVORES

About 23000 years ago, when wolves were hunting in packs and humans were living in groups, they fell into a symbiotic relationship where man & wolf co-evolved ever after.Probably this is the longest co-evolution that has ever occurred dating back to days with no calendars…

Canus Lupus Familiaris(Dogs) are descendants of Canus Lupus (Grey Wolves). These wolves, thousands of years ago were formidable predators living in packs, hunting their prey. So was the homo sapiens working in groups for their living.

Evolution happens when members of species overcome the changing and challenging environments around them. Men welcomed friendly & less aggressive wolves for their useful traits like sharp smell, vision, speed etc. Wolves were happy too in this partnership as it had guaranteed easy meals and a safe home.

Eventually, over thousands of years man was able to change some of indigenous traits and appearance of dogs as per his needs to have a shepherd, a hunter, or a retriever. However, the extent of changes is only within the limits of what was existing in the genes already. For example, we cannot make a green Dog or a Dog that can draw!

Fundamentally, although Dogs have evolved through thousands of years, genetically Dogs are still wolves. It is scientifically proven that a Dog’s genotype varies only by a 0.2% from that of a grey wolf.

Hence it is wrong to think that the Dog's diet should be significantly different from wolves.

Afterall, DOGS ARE CAPTIVE CARNIVORES
