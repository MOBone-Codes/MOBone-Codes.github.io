Most Dogs love MOBone when thawed overnight in the refrigerator. Alternatively you can,

  • Place the frozen MOBone in the feeding bowl
  • Add about 300ml of boiling water per 225 Gms of MOBone.
  • Add the coconut oil bone.
  • Stir and let it sit for 5-8 mins before your serve.

Because we don’t use harmful preservatives! We rely on below zero temperature to preserve the food, good for at least 6 months.

Every Dog’s appetite vastly vary, based on factors like age, activity, habitat etc. So no thumb rule to follow. Typically an Adult Dog needs ~3% of his ideal weight, whereas fast growing puppies intake ~6% of their own weight.

We advice to start off with the reference chart below and regulate based on your Dog’s needs.

Weight of the Dog Feed Qty (Grams per Day) for Puppy Feed Qty(Grams per Day) for Adult
5 Kgs 300 150
10 Kgs 600 300
15 Kgs 900 450

Diet of the nursing bitches and ailing aged Dogs differ from the above recommendation.

No defined rules here. But ideally an adult Dog can be fed once a day whereas puppies can be fed 4 meals with 3 hour interval between meals.

Note :

  • Avoid rough play and workouts (Fetch, Climbing, Swimming, Etc.) 45 minutes prior and post meal time.
  • Do ensure your Dog has access to fresh, clean water at all times.
